# The Dog Song

Woof woof, I greet the day,
Tail a-wagging all the way.
Paws on pavement, nose to ground,
Best companion to be found.

Chase the squirrel, chase the ball,
Bark at shadows on the wall.
Curl up close when day is done,
Loyal friend beneath the sun.

Woof woof woof, woof woof woof,
Every day's a brand new roof
(to sleep beneath, with you nearby)
Woof woof woof beneath the sky.

Fetch the stick and bring it back,
Ears alert for every crack.
Guard the house with all my might,
Then snore softly through the night.

Woof woof, I greet the day,
Best of friends in every way.
