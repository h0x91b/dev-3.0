Add configurable terminal keymap presets: "Convenient" (Cmd+W kills pane), "iTerm2" (Cmd+W/D/Shift+D/T/[/]), and "Native tmux" (no shortcuts). The active preset is selected in Global Settings and persisted across sessions. iTerm2 preset maps macOS shortcuts to the equivalent tmux operations (kill-pane, split-window, new-window, select-pane). Also adds nextPane, prevPane, and newWindow to the tmuxAction RPC.
