Handle unhandled RPC timeout rejections. Added a Proxy wrapper around `api.request` in the renderer that attaches a safety-net `.catch()` to every RPC call, preventing "RPC request timed out" from becoming unhandled promise rejections. The `unhandledrejection` handler in analytics now detects RPC timeouts, calls `event.preventDefault()` to suppress the scary error, and logs them as warnings instead.
