Redesigned the tmux pane control bar with custom SVG icons and expanded it from 4 to 10 buttons. New layout preset buttons (tiled, even-rows, even-columns, main+bottom, main+right), a close-pane button, a fullscreen/zoom button, and a cycle-layouts button.
