**Critical bug fix:** Shift+Tab and Shift+Enter were broken in the terminal. ghostty-web's InputHandler has a bug where Shift+functional keys are treated the same as unmodified keys — Shift+Tab sent `\t` instead of CSI Z (`\x1b[Z`), and Shift+Enter sent `\r` instead of `\n`. This broke mode switching (Shift+Tab) and multi-line input (Shift+Enter) in claude-code and other terminal tools. Added `attachCustomKeyEventHandler` workaround in TerminalView to intercept both keys and send correct sequences.
