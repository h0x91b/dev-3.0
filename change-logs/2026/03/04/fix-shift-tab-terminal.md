**Critical bug fix:** Shift+Tab was completely broken in the terminal. ghostty-web's InputHandler has a bug where both Tab and Shift+Tab produce the same `\t` character instead of sending the correct CSI Z (`\x1b[Z`) backtab sequence. This broke Shift+Tab mode switching in claude-code and other terminal tools. Added a `attachCustomKeyEventHandler` workaround in TerminalView to intercept Shift+Tab and send the proper escape sequence.
