When a macOS task hangs on `fetching-origin` for longer than 60 seconds, a small non-blocking popover now appears next to the stuck card explaining the most likely cause — Full Disk Access being silently revoked for git/tmux child processes — and offering a one-click deep-link to the macOS System Settings → Full Disk Access pane. Cancel keeps the existing cancel-preparation behaviour. Threshold can be overridden via the `DEV3_STUCK_PREP_THRESHOLD_SEC` environment variable. The previous centred blocking modal has been replaced.
