Fixed the tmux PATH shim (`~/.dev3.0/bin/tmux`) symlinking onto itself for users without the vendored tmux@3.6 keg (e.g. after an in-app update, which cannot install brew dependencies) — every tmux spawn then failed with ELOOP and no session could start or resume. Binary resolution now dereferences the shim to its real target, refuses to point the shim at itself, and removes an already-broken shim at startup so affected installs self-heal. Also logs a warning when the selected tmux is the known-bad 3.7, and documents the `brew trust h0x91b/dev3` step newer Homebrew requires for tap installs/upgrades.
