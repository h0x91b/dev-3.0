Pin tmux to 3.6a: tmux 3.7 clients busy-spin at 100% CPU on a congested server socket, freezing the UI for 10-35s. The Homebrew cask/formula now depend on the keg-only h0x91b/dev3/tmux@3.6, the app prefers that keg over PATH, a `~/.dev3.0/bin/tmux` shim keeps agent-issued tmux commands on the same binary, and a live-server compatibility probe keeps existing sessions alive across the upgrade.
