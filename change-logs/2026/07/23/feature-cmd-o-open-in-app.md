Short: Open in… popup with Cmd+O

Press ⌘O (macOS) or Ctrl+O (Linux) to pop up a searchable launcher of your installed apps (Finder, VS Code, Cursor, Ghostty, …, plus any custom apps you added). Type to filter, use ↑↓, or press 1–9, and it opens the current project — or the active task's worktree — in that app. Works both on the Kanban board and inside a task.
