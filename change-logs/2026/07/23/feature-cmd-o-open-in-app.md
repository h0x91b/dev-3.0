Short: Open in… popup with Cmd+O

Press ⌘O (macOS) or Ctrl+O (Linux) to pop up a picker of your installed apps (Finder, VS Code, Cursor, Ghostty, …) and open the current project — or the active task's worktree — in the one you click. Works both on the Kanban board and inside a task.
