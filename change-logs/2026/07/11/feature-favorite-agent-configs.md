Added Favorites to the agent launch picker: star your go-to provider/model/mode combos and pick them in one click. A compact "Favorites" column sits at the start of the picker on Launch, Spawn Agent, and Bug Hunters — its star fills when the current combo is saved, and clicking it opens a menu to save the current combo or apply/remove a saved one. Favorites are capped at 10, ordered by how often you use them, and the least-used one is dropped when you star an eleventh.
