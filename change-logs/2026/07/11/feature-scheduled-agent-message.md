Added "Send later" scheduled messages: queue a one-shot prompt to a task's live agent to be delivered at a wall-clock time or after a delay. Reach it from the inspector Session bar, the task-card menu, the browser/touch terminal composer, or `dev3 message [--in <dur> | --at <hh:mm>] "text"` (bare form sends immediately). The pending queue shows as a countdown chip both on the board card and in the open-task Session bar, each with cancel / send-now; the compose dialog accepts dropped or pasted images (uploaded into the worktree). Unresolvable or offline-late deliveries notify instead of silently failing.
