Added an opt-in "Fable 5 (cost trick)" agent preset that routes Claude through the local pxpipe token-saving proxy (ANTHROPIC_BASE_URL). It appears as its own greyed-out Model choice in the launch picker while disabled; clicking it links to a new Global Settings "Token-saving proxy" section that checks npx, detects the port 47821 owner (ours vs foreign), and starts/stops the proxy. The section always shows a credit link to the upstream repo, and surfaces the proxy dashboard link (savings breakdown) inside the status block once the proxy is running. The trick and proxy are the work of the pxpipe team (github.com/teamchong/pxpipe); it is experimental and ships off by default — slower, but typically halves request cost or more, with virtually unchanged accuracy.
