Cmd/Ctrl+K is now a "Go to project or task" palette with a mode toggle: Projects, Both (projects and tasks together), and Tasks (across every project). Tap Shift or click the footer mode switcher to cycle. Task rows group by recency — Today / Yesterday / This week / Older, most-recently-updated first, with fuzzy search, a status dot, and a project badge. It opens in your last-used mode (default Projects). Also reachable from Cmd+Shift+P and the View menu.
