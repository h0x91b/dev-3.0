Tmux toolbar icons now animate on hover, acting out the operation they trigger: splits saw their divider across, cycle-layout swaps the panes, zoom inflates the pane, close draws the X and blinks the pane out, layout presets draw their dividers, and the mobile New Window icon pops into existence. Shared glyph components live in TmuxIcons.tsx; a standalone design reference page was added at design/tmux-icons-animated.html.
