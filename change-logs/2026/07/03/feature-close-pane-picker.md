Close Pane is now a two-step visual picker instead of blindly killing the active pane: click it, then hover the exact tmux split you mean and click to close it. Each pane shows an inset marching-ants marquee — neutral blue while idle, turning red with a "Close" chip on hover — under a readable mode hint; Esc cancels. The last-pane teardown still asks for confirmation, and narrow/mobile keeps the direct-kill fallback.
