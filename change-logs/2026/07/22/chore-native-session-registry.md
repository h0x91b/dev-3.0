Added a persistent, multi-session native-terminal registry (dev-internal groundwork for the tmux-removal roadmap): start/list/status/attach/stop several detached native sessions by stable id, with restart reattach, per-session start locking, passive stale/reused-PID detection, and token-matched cleanup. It is an additive parallel namespace under ~/.dev3.0/native-sessions/ and is not wired into any production UI, CLI, RPC, or tmux flow.
