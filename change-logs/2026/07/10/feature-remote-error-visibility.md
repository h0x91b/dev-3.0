Remote and mobile-browser sessions now surface faults in the UI instead of hanging on a blank "Loading…" with no way to see what broke. A top-level error boundary renders render/lifecycle crashes as a readable, copyable screen; the loading screen names its phase (connecting, authenticating, loading) and, after a timeout, offers Retry/Reload with the actual error; uncaught errors, unhandled rejections, and WebSocket/RPC failures are collected into an in-UI diagnostics panel reachable from a small floating indicator that only appears in remote mode when something has actually gone wrong. A static pre-React loader also replaces the initial blank flash.
