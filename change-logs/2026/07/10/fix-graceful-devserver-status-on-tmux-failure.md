`dev3 dev-server status` no longer hard-crashes with a raw `posix_spawn ENOENT` when tmux can't be launched (usually macOS Full Disk Access lost). It now degrades gracefully — printing the last-known state as "unknown" plus a clear diagnostic that points at re-adding dev3 to Full Disk Access — and `start`/`restart` surface the same actionable message instead of the cryptic spawn error.
