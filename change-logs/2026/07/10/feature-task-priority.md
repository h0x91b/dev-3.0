Tasks now have a priority (P0 highest … P4 lowest, default P2) shown as a colored badge on every card — a red → orange → yellow → green → grey heat ramp by importance; click it to change. The board columns and the active-tasks sidebar sort by priority first, so the most important work is always on top, and dragging a card into a higher/lower band re-prioritizes it. A colored priority filter joins the label filter on a single-line, horizontally-scrollable filter row, and `dev3 task update --priority P0..P4` lets you delegate prioritizing to an agent.
