Fixed Ctrl+C being completely ignored when the app runs under `bun run dev`. Two layered causes: the shell-env bootstrap's interactive zsh stole the terminal's foreground process group and left it dead (SIGINT was delivered to nobody) — the shell now runs with job control disabled (`+m`); and the unified quit gate intercepted signal-initiated quits behind the GUI confirmation dialog — SIGINT/SIGTERM now bypass the dialog and shut down gracefully with full cleanup.
