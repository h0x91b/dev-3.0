Fixed multi-second UI freezes (frozen terminals stuck on "Connecting...") caused by synchronous process spawns blocking the main event loop. The port-scanner and resource-monitor pollers ran `lsof`, `ps`, and two `tmux list-panes` per session every 10 seconds via spawnSync — with many active sessions under load this stalled the loop for seconds (loop-monitor recorded up to 91 minutes of cumulative stalls in one day). All process-inspection primitives are now async, per-session tmux calls are batched into a single `list-panes -a`, the `codex --version` probe is cached instead of re-spawned on every task launch, and slow spawnSync calls (≥250ms) are now logged with their command for future field diagnosis. Also stopped the caffeinate sleep-inhibit from retry-spamming ENOENT errors every 10 seconds.
