Mobile remote mode overhaul: sessions now ride an HttpOnly cookie backed by a disk-persisted secret, so your phone stays signed in across desktop app restarts and overnight idle (24h rolling), and a dead session shows a clear "scan a fresh QR" screen instead of an infinite reconnect spinner while genuine network blips keep auto-retrying. The Android Back button now works like a native app — it closes the top sheet or modal first, then navigates back in-app, and asks "press Back again to exit" at the root — and on mobile the UI enters fullscreen on your first tap, with a Fullscreen toggle in the mobile menu.
