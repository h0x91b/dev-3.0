The Active Tasks sidebar "zoom" icon is now a VS Code-style panel toggle, with a matching counterpart in the fullscreen task toolbar that brings the panel back. The terminal fullscreen toggle got a new macOS-style diagonal-arrows icon and an accent tint, and the tmux pane zoom control now shows a magnifier over the split layout instead of the inflating frame. All native tooltips in the sidebar zone and the pane un-zoom badge were migrated to the styled Tooltip, and the redundant sidebar-hide discovery tip was removed.
