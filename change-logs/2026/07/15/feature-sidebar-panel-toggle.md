The Active Tasks sidebar "zoom" icon is now a VS Code-style panel toggle, with a matching counterpart in the fullscreen task toolbar that brings the panel back. The terminal fullscreen toggle got a new macOS-style diagonal-arrows icon and an accent tint so the feature is discoverable next to its grey neighbours. All native tooltips in the sidebar zone (scope toggles, filter funnel, project badge, agent summary, status age, ports) were migrated to the styled two-tier Tooltip, and the redundant sidebar-hide discovery tip was removed.
