Short: Claude Opus 5 is the new default

Claude Opus 5 joins the built-in Claude presets on the 1M-context alias with Medium/High/X-High effort tiers for Auto and Bypass plus Default, Plan and Accept Edits, and Auto (Opus 5, Medium) is now the default configuration for new launches. Opus 4.8, Sonnet 5, Fable 5 and Opus 4.7 presets are unchanged, and the bare `opus` alias now resolves to Opus 5 on third-party backends such as Bedrock.
