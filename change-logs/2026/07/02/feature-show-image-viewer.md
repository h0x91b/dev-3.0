Added `dev3 show-image <path...>` — agents can now surface images (screenshots, agent-browser captures, rendered charts) to the human in an in-app lightbox bound to the task, with a clickable history (newest shown first). New images raise the task's attention badge and a clickable toast; the viewer auto-opens only when you're already looking at that task (toggle in Settings → Behavior). Images are copied into the worktree `shared-images/` dir. Reopen anytime from the image-count badge in the task inspector.
