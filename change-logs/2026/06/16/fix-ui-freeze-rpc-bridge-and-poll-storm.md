Fixed two UI-freeze classes. The desktop RPC bridge (Electrobun localhost socket) had no reconnect, so after sleep every request could hang for the full timeout with the GUI stuck (terminals kept working) until a force quit — added a liveness-ping watchdog that re-opens the socket and, as a last resort, reloads the webview while bun stays alive. Separately, git-status pollers fired on every open task panel every 15s and all pending timers stampeded on wake; pollers are now visibility-aware (paused while hidden, single refresh on wake) with jitter, and heavy branch-status runs are capped by a global concurrency semaphore.
