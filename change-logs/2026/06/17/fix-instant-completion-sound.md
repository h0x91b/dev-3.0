Completing or cancelling a task now reacts instantly on every surface (board drag, card status menu, info panel, detail modal, terminal toolbar): the card moves optimistically and the completion sound plays the moment you act, instead of waiting for the worktree-cleanup round-trip. All the previously divergent move flows were unified into a single moveTaskToStatus helper so behaviour is identical everywhere. The bun completion sound is now suppressed precisely by task id, so two tasks finishing back-to-back both ring while the local play and its echo never double up.
