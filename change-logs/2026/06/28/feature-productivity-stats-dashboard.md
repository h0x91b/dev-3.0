Added a Productivity Stats dashboard ("Velocity Cockpit") — a stylish full-screen view that proves how much you ship: tasks completed over time, lines of code changed, projects/tasks/agents counts, streaks, busiest project, and per-project and per-agent breakdowns, with a day/week/month/all time-range switch. Built around the speedometer gauge, plus custom SVG bar/line charts. Lines-of-code are captured per task at completion time (before the worktree is removed) so they survive cleanup. Each gauge auto-scales to a tight round maximum and paints a red "beat-your-average" zone from your rolling average up to the top, so topping your usual output is visible at a glance. The completed/lines charts use recharts (real axes, hover tooltips), the per-agent split is a donut, and the per-project breakdown uses LED-style segmented bars. Reachable from the Dashboard card and a speedometer button in the top header. The screen boots up alive — gauge needles sweep from zero and numbers count up — and adds a GitHub-style year-long contribution heatmap, lifetime shipping medals (100/500/1000…) with progress to the next, a dynamic "momentum" headline that calls out when you're on fire, and a friendly placeholder while diff tracking warms up. All motion respects prefers-reduced-motion.
