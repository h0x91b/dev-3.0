On a narrow viewport the terminal pane carousel gains a "zoom-out" overview: a grid button opens a bottom sheet that draws a spatial mini-map of the active tmux window's panes by their real geometry (like the boxes `dev3 ui state` prints) — tap a box to jump to and zoom that pane. Backed by a new `tmuxLayout` RPC that returns pane geometry plus the window list (the foundation for a future windows switcher and +split/+window).
