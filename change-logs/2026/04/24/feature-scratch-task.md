Added **Scratch Task** button to the New Task modal. Creates a todo task with a `Scratch — HH:mm` placeholder title and immediately opens the Launch Task dialog so you can pick the agent/config. The agent is launched with no initial prompt — it greets you, asks what you need, and rewrites the task title/overview/labels itself once you tell it. Handy when you just want a sandbox terminal and haven't formulated the task yet.
