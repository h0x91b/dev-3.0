Fix stale content in the inline diff viewer after a branch rebase or fresh refetch. The underlying `@git-diff-view/core` library caches its internal `File` instances keyed only by the `uuid` we pass to the `DiffFile` constructor; reusing a stable file-path uuid across re-renders returned the previously built `File` with the old content even when fresh hunks and content were supplied. We now mix a content hash into the uuid so the cache invalidates whenever the diff payload changes, and added a regression test that pins the library behaviour and covers the fix.
