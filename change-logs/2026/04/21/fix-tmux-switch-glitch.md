Fixed the garbled/overlapping terminal content and the flicker that appeared when switching between tasks. Three changes: the PTY server no longer runs a `capture-pane` replay on WebSocket reconnect (its content was at tmux's stale pre-resize dimensions); `TaskTerminal` is now keyed by `taskId` so a task switch starts from a fresh `ptyUrl` state instead of briefly remounting `TerminalView` with the leaving task's URL; and the terminal's resize dance nudges the row count instead of the column count, so the two paints share the same text wrapping and the transition is visually imperceptible.
